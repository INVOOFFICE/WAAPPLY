How to Integrate AI Into Your Daily Entrepreneur Routine to Gain 2 Hours Per Day

2 hours per day recovered thanks to AI equals 500 hours per year. Here's how to achieve it concretely starting tomorrow.

Open this guide on the homepage (expandable with other guides)

Two hours per day. That's the time entrepreneurs who use AI intelligently report recovering each day for higher-value tasks. Over a year, that's the equivalent of 12 weeks of work. Here's how to build this routine. Morning (30 minutes of AI replaces 1.5 hours of classic work): start the day with an AI brief. Paste unprocessed emails and ask for a summary of priority actions. Generate the day's plan based on your weekly goals. Draft responses to complex emails with AI's help — a draft in 30 seconds that you revise in 2 minutes rather than 15 minutes of writing from scratch

During the day (punctual use)

whenever a writing task comes up (email, commercial proposal, LinkedIn post), use AI for the first draft. For every complex problem, ask AI to list possible approaches before deciding. For every meeting, generate a structured agenda and key questions to ask

Evening (15 minutes of AI to capitalize on the day)

dictate or type the day's summary and ask AI to extract decisions made, follow-up actions, and insights. Prepare tomorrow's priorities

Specific tasks where AI makes the biggest difference

writing emails and commercial proposals (saves 30-45 min/day), information research and monitoring (saves 20-30 min/day), creating presentations and reports (saves 45-60 min/week), meeting preparation (saves 15-20 min/meeting)

The initial investment

one week to learn how to properly formulate requests. The learning curve is fast — after 7 days of regular use, productivity gains become obvious
