Midjourney vs DALL-E vs Firefly: Which AI Image Tool to Choose in 2026?

AI image generators have exploded. Here's how to choose between Midjourney, DALL-E, Adobe Firefly and others based on your actual use case.

Open this guide on the homepage (expandable with other guides)

AI image generation has become an indispensable professional tool in 2026. But with the multiplication of available options, choosing the right tool can be confusing. Here's an honest comparison of the main platforms. Midjourney is unanimously recognized for the artistic quality of its renders — it's the choice for creators who want impressive, stylized and unique images

Its strengths

exceptional visual quality, varied artistic styles, and an active community sharing prompts

Its limits

Discord interface not very intuitive for beginners, difficult to precisely control for technical uses, paid subscription required. DALL-E 3 (integrated into ChatGPT) excels at understanding complex instructions and generating images with precise text inside them. Ideal for specific and technical requests. Accessible via ChatGPT, making it very practical. Adobe Firefly is the choice for design professionals for its native integration in the Adobe suite (Photoshop, Illustrator), its clear commercial license, and its training on legal data. Perfect for designers who want to integrate AI into their existing workflow. Stable Diffusion (open-source) is for technical users who want total control, the ability to run locally without subscription, and to train their own models. High learning curve but maximum freedom. Google Imagen via Gemini is rapidly improving in quality and benefits from native integration in the Google ecosystem

How to choose

for visual marketing and social media → Midjourney. For documents and presentations with text → DALL-E. For design professionals → Adobe Firefly. For developers and enthusiasts → Stable Diffusion. Mastering visual image description (visual prompt) is the key skill regardless of which tool you choose
