Creating Videos With AI in 2026: Tools, Techniques and Complete Workflow

AI video creation exploded in 2026. Here's how to produce professional videos without a camera or a team.

Open this guide on the homepage (expandable with other guides)

Video is the king format in 2026, but creating quality video content until recently required a team, expensive equipment and hours of editing. AI has radically changed the game. Here's the complete overview of available tools and techniques

Script generation

ChatGPT or Claude to write engagement-optimized scripts with narrative structure, rhythm, and calls to action

Voice generation

ElevenLabs lets you clone a real voice or use AI voices of near-human quality in many languages. Suno and Udio for custom background music

AI avatar creation

platforms like HeyGen or Synthesia let you create realistic AI avatars that read your script — ideal for training, presentations and educational content

Video sequence generation

Runway ML, Sora (OpenAI) and Kling let you generate video sequences from text descriptions. In 2026, quality has sufficiently improved for commercial uses

AI-assisted editing

tools like Descript let you edit video by editing the transcript text — deleting a passage in the script removes the corresponding video sequence. CapCut integrates powerful AI functions available for free

A complete workflow to create a YouTube video in 2 hours

write the script with AI (20 min), generate voiceover (5 min), create or select visuals with AI (30 min), assemble and edit (40 min), generate AI-optimized thumbnail and title (15 min), publish and schedule (10 min) charismatic on-screen presence, natural humor, and authentic emotional connection with an audience.
