AI Copywriting in 2026: How to Write Texts That Actually Sell

Copywriting is the art of writing to sell. AI democratizes it — but the fundamentals still matter. The complete guide.

Open this guide on the homepage (expandable with other guides)

Copywriting — the art of writing texts that convince and convert — is one of the most valued skills in marketing. In 2026, AI allows anyone to access professional-quality texts, provided they master the fundamental principles

Copywriting structures that work and that AI masters perfectly

AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action) — the classic formula for any commercial message. PAS (Problem, Agitation, Solution) — start by identifying the prospect's problem, amplify their pain, then present the solution. FAB (Features, Advantages, Benefits) — not the product's characteristics but the concrete benefits for the client. BEFORE/AFTER/BRIDGE — describe the prospect's life before, after, and present your product as the bridge between the two

How to use AI for copywriting

give maximum context about the target (age, problems, objections, aspirations), specify the channel (email, landing page, Facebook ad, SMS), indicate the tone (professional, conversational, urgent, inspiring), and always ask it to generate several variations

Specific prompts for copywriting

'Write 5 versions of hooks for a Facebook ad targeting [audience] with the problem [problem] and the solution [solution], using the AIDA formula.' AI excels at quickly generating variations to test. The judgment on what will resonate with your specific audience remains human. Systematically A/B test the generated texts to identify what truly converts

The golden rule

AI can write the words, but deep understanding of your target client is what transforms a correct text into one that actually sells
