The 8 Biggest AI Trends Changing the World in 2026

AI is evolving at a dizzying speed. Here are the 8 major trends of 2026 and what they concretely mean for you.

Open this guide on the homepage (expandable with other guides)

2026 marks an unprecedented acceleration in the development and adoption of artificial intelligence. Here are the 8 trends redefining our relationship with technology

Agentic AI

AIs no longer just answer questions — they plan and execute complex tasks autonomously. 'AI agents' can now browse the web, fill out forms, send emails, and manage entire workflows with minimal supervision

Generalized multimodality

the most advanced models simultaneously process text, image, audio and video. Showing a photo of a problem and asking for a solution has become as natural as describing it

On-device AI

high-end smartphones in 2026 embed AI models that work entirely offline — guaranteed data protection and performance

Personalization at scale

AI systems learn each user's preferences to offer completely personalized experiences — content, advertising, education, health

Photorealistic video generation

the boundary between real video and AI-generated video has become very difficult to distinguish, creating both creative opportunities and disinformation challenges

AI in healthcare

assisted diagnosis, drug discovery, personalized patient monitoring — medical AI is concretely saving lives in 2026 training an AI model on one's own data has become accessible to non-experts, allowing hypersocialized tools. the EU, US and China have adopted different regulatory frameworks, creating a patchwork of rules shaping innovation. AI skills are becoming as important as basic computer skills were in the 2000s. Adapting now is an investment in your own employability and entrepreneurial capability.
