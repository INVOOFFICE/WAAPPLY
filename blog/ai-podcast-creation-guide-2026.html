Create a Podcast With AI in 2026: From Script to Distribution, The Complete Guide

Podcasting is one of the most powerful formats to build an audience. AI makes it accessible to anyone with something to say.

Open this guide on the homepage (expandable with other guides)

Podcasting is experiencing continuous growth in 2026 and AI has made its creation accessible to anyone who has something to say. Here's the complete guide to launching a quality podcast with AI's help

Ideation and positioning with AI

define your niche, target audience, and differentiating angle with AI's help

Prompt

'Help me define the unique positioning of a podcast on [topic] for an audience of [target] in a market where [competitors] already exist.' Script creation: AI generates structured episode plans, interview questions, transitions between segments, and impactful introductions/conclusions. A 30-minute episode can be scripted in 20 minutes with AI

Audio production

tools like Adobe Podcast automatically clean background noise and improve audio quality — transforming an amateur recording into studio sound. ElevenLabs to generate musical intros/outros or even create an AI voice if you don't want to record yourself

AI-assisted editing

Descript automatically transcribes audio and lets you edit by cutting words in the text. 'Filler words' (um, uh) can be removed with a click

Distribution and promotion

automatically generate show notes, summaries, key quotes, and social posts from the episode transcript. Tools like Castmagic do all of this automatically

Podcast SEO

optimize episode titles and descriptions for search engines with AI's help launch a professional podcast in less than a week and maintain weekly publication with 3 to 4 hours of work per episode instead of 10 to 15 without AI.
